In software, we have a ritual called "Hello World." It is the first line of code you write to prove a system works.

After decades of building complex systems I realized "making it work" is only baseline competency. Real challenges and real beauty lie in **craft**.

### The Canvas
My engineering journey is a long arc across continents and entire software stacks.

I spent years deep in the trenches of **Multimedia, Streaming, Networking, Operating Systems and Embedded Systems**. These are my roots. It is where I learned to build hardware-level primitives. Today I apply that hard-won wisdom to guide new frontiers: **Edge AI and NPUs**.

For me these aren't just isolated domains like **IoT** or **Devices**. They are tools we use to build modern infrastructure.

### Strategic Leadership
I charted an impact-driven course through corporate hierarchies. I spent several years deeply embedded in Management Roles. I managed several teams, guided complex product strategies and mentored engineering talent. 

I have seen this industry from both sides: high-level management and deep technical implementation. 

Titles are temporary. Impact is permanent.

### Why I Am Writing
I am building `tribhuwan.dev` as a **digital garden** to share:

1.  **Hard Tech:** Deep dives into Multimedia, Systems Architecture and guiding Edge AI Initiatives.
2.  **Career Maps:** Unvarnished truths about navigating engineering careers. I want to share strategies I used (starting from humble backgrounds to mastering complex role transitions) to actively mentor next generations so they can walk that path with confidence.
3.  **Philosophy:** How to think like an architect and lead with empathy.

### To the Next Generation
If you are just starting out or seeking guidance in the messy middle of your career: **I am writing this for you.**

Technology is not just conquest. It is an art form. Let's build something lasting.
